About

Introducing Matthew’s Angel’s Innovation: Students of Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship will introduce Empanada Pizza. Empanada pizza is a unique and delicious fusion dish that combines the flavors and elements of traditional Latin American empanadas with the popular Italian pizza concept.

Empanada pizza is a savory and innovative dish that brings together the best of two culinary worlds. It features a crispy and flaky empanada crust filled with a delectable combination of Italian pizza toppings, such as Ham and cheese, savory tomato sauce,The result is a handheld meal that offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, perfect for those looking to experience a unique twist on classic comfort foods.

Empanada pizza is not just a meal; it's a culinary adventure that promises to delight the taste buds of those seeking a new and exciting dining experience.
